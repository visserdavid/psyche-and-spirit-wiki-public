**Summary**: The framework describing the human body as embedded in a multilayered energy field that contains the full record of a person's life experiences and mediates between biography and biological health.

## Overview

Energy anatomy refers to the architecture of the human energy system — the field surrounding and permeating the body that, according to energy medicine, serves as the primary medium through which life experience is converted into biological reality. It is the structural basis for [biography is biology](biography-is-biology.md).

[Myss](../thinkers/caroline-myss.md) describes the energy field as extending outward from the body approximately to the distance of outstretched arms in all directions. It functions as:

- An **information centre**: recording every experience, relationship, belief, memory, and emotional investment.
- A **communication system**: transmitting and receiving energy from other people and environments through what Myss calls "energy circuits."
- A **health barometer**: the first site at which stress and dis-ease appear, before they manifest in the physical body.

## The Energy Field

The field is not empty space. It carries encoded impressions of a person's entire biography. [Myss](../thinkers/caroline-myss.md) — as a medical intuitive — reads this field to identify not only existing illness but stress patterns that are precursors to illness. She receives impressions as images, sensations, and symbolic information that she then interprets in anatomical and biographical terms.

Key properties of the energy field:
- **It does not lie**: even when a person consciously withholds information, their energy field transmits the truth of their experience.
- **It is bidirectional**: we both project energy into the world and draw energy in from others and environments.
- **Depletion is cumulative**: prolonged investment of energy in fear, resentment, grief, or attachment gradually depletes the field, weakening specific body regions and increasing susceptibility to illness.

## Energy Circuits

Myss introduces the concept of **energy circuits** — pathways through which energy flows between a person and their "power targets" (people, substances, roles, or symbols they have invested with power over their sense of self). When energy circuits run to an external target (a controlling spouse, an addiction, a lost status), they drain the body's own field.

She uses the term **acquisitioners** to describe people whose energy circuits are so strongly attached to external power targets that their health becomes contingent on the availability of those targets. The case of Julie — whose energy circuits ran to her husband, leaving her body depleted — illustrates the biological consequences.

## Seven Power Centres

The energy field is organised around seven primary centres, corresponding to the [chakras](chakra-system.md). Each centre governs specific bodily regions, specific life lessons, and specific emotional/biographical concerns:

- **First centre** (base of spine): tribal identity, survival, bones, immune system
- **Second centre** (lower abdomen): relationships, sexuality, money, creativity, lower organs
- **Third centre** (solar plexus): personal power, self-esteem, stomach, pancreas, liver
- **Fourth centre** (heart): love, forgiveness, heart, lungs, breasts
- **Fifth centre** (throat): will, truth, expression, thyroid, throat, mouth
- **Sixth centre** (third eye): mind, intuition, perception, brain, eyes
- **Seventh centre** (crown): spirit, consciousness, connection to the Divine

## Scientific Context

Myss situates energy anatomy within a broader scientific framing:
- Quantum physics confirms the vibratory essence of all matter.
- The body generates measurable electricity; living tissue generates energy fields.
- [Candace Pert](../thinkers/candace-pert.md)'s neuropeptide research demonstrates that emotions have molecular correlates distributed throughout body tissue.
- Researchers like Elmer Green (biofeedback) and Barbara Ann Brennan (healers working with the energy field) provide precedents.

Myss acknowledges that conventional medicine does not recognise or measure the energy field she describes, and that "no instrument can evaluate one person's frequency blocks."

[Shealy](../thinkers/norman-shealy.md)'s *Sacred Healing* provides additional scientific grounding through [bioelectromagnetism](bioelectromagnetism.md) — particularly [Robert Becker](../thinkers/robert-becker.md)'s demonstration that living tissue is a semiconductor that generates and responds to bioelectric fields. Shealy proposes [DHEA](dhea-and-vitality.md) as a measurable biochemical correlate of the energy field's vitality. The quantum physicists' finding that DNA resonates at 52–78 gigahertz supports the view that living systems are organised by high-frequency electromagnetic fields extending into ranges not previously studied in medicine.

Shealy also documented that healer Ostad Hadi Parvarandeh could measurably alter patients' EEG patterns at a distance — without contact, without patient knowledge — suggesting that the energy field is capable of influence across space. See [Prayer and Healing](prayer-and-healing.md).

## Brennan's Seven-Layer Anatomy: The Most Detailed Model

[Barbara Ann Brennan](../thinkers/barbara-ann-brennan.md) provides the most structurally specific account of the human energy field in the ingested literature. Her [seven-layer auric field model](seven-layer-auric-field.md) describes seven distinct interpenetrating bodies extending from the physical body outward, each corresponding to one chakra and one domain of experience:

- **Layer 1 (Etheric)**: tissue matrix template, physical sensation
- **Layer 2 (Emotional)**: feelings, emotional life
- **Layer 3 (Mental)**: thoughts, thought forms
- **Layer 4 (Astral)**: love, relationship cords, heart connection
- **Layer 5 (Etheric template)**: blueprint for the etheric body; sound heals here
- **Layer 6 (Celestial)**: spiritual ecstasy, universal love
- **Layer 7 (Ketheric template)**: causal body, life plan, past-life bands

This model extends Myss's framework from a diagnostic/biographical tool to a therapeutic anatomy. Where Myss reads the energy field to identify the emotional and biographical roots of illness, Brennan reads it to identify which layer is distorted — and then restructures it through specific healing techniques appropriate to that layer's frequency.

Brennan's model is grounded in laboratory evidence: Burr & Northrop's L-fields (1939), Inyushin's bioplasma research (1950s–1970s), and particularly Hunt & Bruyere's UCLA study (1977), which showed that Bruyere's real-time auric colour observations correlated with instrument-measured body-surface frequencies in the range of 250–2000 Hz — providing partial empirical support for the existence of a layered, colour-coded energy field.

## Brennan's Extended Model: Four Dimensions of Being (Light Emerging, 1993)

In *Light Emerging*, Brennan extends the energy anatomy model beyond the seven auric layers to a [four-dimensional framework](four-dimensions-of-being.md):

1. **Physical world** — the material body
2. **Auric level** — the seven-layer HEF
3. **Haric level** — the [hara line](hara-level.md): the dimension of intentionality and life purpose, with three structural points (Individuation Point, Soul Seat, Tan Tien)
4. **Core star level** — the [core star](core-star.md): the divine essence; the source of all creative energy

This vertical extension addresses a clinical gap in the *Hands of Light* model: some illnesses and some people do not respond sustainably to auric-level healing because their root dysfunction lies at the haric level (disconnection from life purpose) or core star level (disconnection from divine essence). The hara line provides the energetic anatomy of intention; the core star provides the anatomy of the source.

## Johari's Prana-Nadi Anatomy: The Traditional Substrate

[Harish Johari](../thinkers/harish-johari.md)'s [*Chakras: Energy Centers of Transformation*](../sources/chakras-energy-centers.md) provides the traditional Hindu-Tantric energy anatomy that underlies the Western chakra models. Where Myss and Brennan work with the chakra system as a map of biography and illness, Johari describes the subtle-body infrastructure through which the chakras are activated: the [prana and nadi system](prana-and-nadis.md).

In this view, the body operates at two levels simultaneously: the material level (five elements, seven dhatus/body tissues) and the subtle level (prana life force, manas mind, buddhi intellect, ahamkara I-sense, chitta feeling-self). The subtle level is mapped by 72,000 nadis (energy channels), of which three are primary: Ida (lunar, left), Pingala (solar, right), and Sushumna (central). Sushumna contains three nested inner channels ending in Brahma Nadi — the actual passage of [Kundalini](kundalini.md) energy in its ascent to liberation.

This framework adds a vertical dimension to the energy anatomy map: whereas Myss's energy circuits run horizontally (from person to power-target) and Brennan's auric layers run outward (from dense-physical to subtle-celestial), Johari's nadi system runs along the spine and describes how prana flows through the body in cycles (Svara Yoga, the nasal breath science) and how Kundalini's vertical ascent transforms each chakra in turn.

Johari also names the [five koshas](koshas.md) (sheaths: matter, vital air, mental, wisdom, bliss) as the levels through which Yoga practices operate — a five-layer anatomy parallel in spirit to Brennan's seven-layer HEF but from a different tradition and with a liberative rather than therapeutic aim.

## Keleman's Somatic Anatomy: A Parallel and a Contrast

[Stanley Keleman](../thinkers/stanley-keleman.md)'s *Emotional Anatomy* describes the body as organized into tubes, layers, and pouches — a pulsating anatomical system that encodes biographical experience in observable tissue structure. This is a secular parallel to Myss's energy anatomy: both describe the body as a medium through which lived experience becomes biology. But the frameworks diverge in their claims:

| Aspect | Myss's Energy Anatomy | Brennan's Seven-Layer Anatomy | Keleman's Somatic Anatomy |
|--------|----------------------|-------------------------------|--------------------------|
| The medium | Subtle energy field | Structured seven-layer HEF | Physical tissue, pulsation |
| The record | Encoded in chakras and energy circuits | Encoded in specific layers, chakras, life bands | Encoded in structural shape, muscular tone |
| Readable by | Medical intuitives | HSP practitioners | Somatic observers |
| Treatable by | Healer must address the source | Layer-specific healing techniques | HOw process, somatic reorganisation |
| Validated by | Experiential/clinical | Clinical + partial laboratory | Clinical/observational |

Keleman's contribution strengthens the cross-framework case for biography-as-biology: where Myss and Shealy approach the same principle from spiritual and bioelectromagnetic directions, Keleman arrives at it from developmental anatomy. Brennan adds a fourth non-overlapping framework — direct perceptual anatomy of the energy field, with partial laboratory support. Four frameworks converge on the same basic claim.

## Joy's Contribution: Clinical Body Mapping and the Transducer Model

[W. Brugh Joy](../thinkers/w-brugh-joy.md) in [*Joy's Way*](../sources/joys-way.md) (1979) provides a clinically grounded independent account of the energy field that extends Myss's and Brennan's frameworks in two directions.

**Empirical grounding**: Joy discovered the energy field through medical practice — passing his hand above patients' bodies during physical examinations and detecting fields he mapped over three months before encountering any knowledge of the chakra system. His detection method (hand scanning) is distinct from Myss's symbolic/remote intuition and Brennan's visual High Sense Perception: it is tactile and close-range, and he trained approximately five hundred people to replicate it with self-reported 99% detection rates in intensive workshop settings.

**Extended mapping**: Joy maps approximately forty field locations including the classical seven chakras plus: a transpersonal point 12–24 inches above the crown, an upper midchest centre, a spleen centre, and field centres at all major joints (hips, knees, elbows, shoulders, hands, feet). He also distinguishes disease fields (anomalous patterns radiating from diseased organs, detectable independently of the chakra centres) from the chakra fields themselves.

**The transducer model**: Joy's most distinctive theoretical contribution is framing the chakra system as a transducer — a device that converts one form of energy into another. He proposes that the chakra system converts energy from higher dimensions (outside the physical plane) into the matter and energy of the physical body, analogous to the way a prism splays light into its constituent frequencies. This frames the energy field not merely as an extension of the physical body but as the interface through which higher-dimensional reality manifests in matter.

**Posthumous persistence and material independence**: Joy reports detecting energy fields in cadavers refrigerated for twenty-four hours after death — which he interprets as evidence that the field is not produced by circulation, nervous system activity, or metabolic heat. If accurate, this would be significant for any biophysical model of the energy field.

## Critique

The concept of the human energy field as described by Myss — a field containing biographical information, readable by intuitives, and causally linked to illness — has not been validated by scientific measurement. Attempts to detect and measure such fields (bioelectric fields, biophotons, etc.) have not produced results consistent with the model Myss describes. Therapeutic Touch and related practices, which claim to work with the human energy field, have been studied in randomised controlled trials with inconsistent and largely inconclusive results.

Shealy's bioelectromagnetic grounding provides a legitimate empirical context for some claims (the body does generate measurable electromagnetic fields; these fields are affected by external EMFs) but does not validate the specific information-carrying, biography-encoding properties that energy medicine attributes to them.

**Note**: Mainstream medicine recognises that the body generates measurable electromagnetic fields (heart, brain, muscles), but these are understood as byproducts of biological processes, not as information-carrying fields encoding biographical history. Becker's work on PEMF fracture healing is accepted; the extension to a full energy medicine model is not.
