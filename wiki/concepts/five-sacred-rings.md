# Five Sacred Rings

**Summary**: Shealy's system of five acupuncture-point circuits, each associated with an essential oil blend, that he claims can raise specific neurochemicals (DHEA, oxytocin, neurotensin, calcitonin) and reduce free radicals — introduced in *Living Bliss* (2014) as the clinical synthesis of thirty years of energy medicine research.

**Sources**:

## Overview

The Five Sacred Rings are five circuits of acupuncture points on the body that [Shealy](../thinkers/norman-shealy.md) developed between 1994 and 2012 through a process he describes as intuition-guided research. Each ring was first revealed to him through what he calls his "inner guide," then given specific point locations he chose based on acupuncture cosmology, and finally tested for measurable biochemical effects.

Each ring can be activated in multiple ways:
1. **GHz stimulation** (Shealy PainPro or similar device) — 3 minutes per pair of points at 75 decibels, DNA frequency 54–78 GHz
2. **Essential oil blend** — 30 seconds to apply to the ring's points; a substitute requiring no equipment
3. **Listening to a CD recording** — guided mental activation
4. **Massage or tapping** of the circuit points

The rings are claimed to work through distinct neurochemical mechanisms, each targeting a different health domain.

## Ring Fire — DHEA and Vitality

**Biological target**: DHEA (dehydroepiandrosterone)

**Claimed effects**: Raises DHEA levels by 60–250% of baseline; treats arthritis, migraines, depression, back pain, neuropathy. In a 12-week trial:
- 70% of arthritis patients who had failed conventional medicine improved
- 75% of migraine patients showed 75% reduction in frequency and severity
- 70% of depression patients improved
- 70% of back pain patients improved
- 80% of neuropathy patients improved (25% reduction in pain; reduced medication needs)

**Circuit points**: Kidney 3 (inside ankles), Conception Vessel 2 (pubic bone center), Conception Vessel 6 (1 inch below umbilicus), Conception Vessel 18 (2 inches above top of breastbone), Bladder 22 (1 inch each side of spine at navel level), Master of Heart 6 (inside wrists), Large Intestine 18 (sides of neck), Governing Vessel 20 (crown of head)

**Oil blend**: Fire Bliss

**Connection to existing wiki**: Extends the [DHEA and Vitality](dhea-and-vitality.md) framework from Sacred Healing; Ring Fire is the acupuncture-based method for DHEA restoration, complementing progesterone cream, magnesium lotion, and the Youth Formula supplement.

## Ring Air — Oxytocin and Neurotensin

**Biological target**: Neurotensin (raised 600%) → Oxytocin

**Claimed effects**: Reduces depression, anxiety, tinnitus, rage; treats autism (dramatically in case reports), ADHD, PTSD, insomnia. The most clinically versatile ring.

**Circuit points**: Spleen 1 (tip of big toenail, inner side), Liver 3 (top of foot, first-second toe web), Stomach 36 (beside shinbone, below knee), Governing Vessel 1 (base of spine), Lung 1 (below collarbone, right and left), Gallbladder 20 (base of skull, both sides), Governing Vessel 16 (top of spine at skull junction), Governing Vessel 20 (crown of head)

**Oil blend**: Air Bliss

**Key discovery**: Shealy found a research paper showing oxytocin is released by neurotensin, and then demonstrated that Ring Air stimulation raises neurotensin by 600%. Direct oxytocin measurement showed increases in 8 of 10 subjects. Air Bliss oil raised oxytocin in 6 of 10.

**Connection to existing wiki**: Directly linked to [Oxytocin and Health](oxytocin-and-health.md); provides the clinical tool for addressing [Electromagnetic Dysthymia](electromagnetic-dysthymia.md) at the oxytocin level.

## Ring Water — Aldosterone and Fluid Balance

**Biological target**: Aldosterone (normalisation)

**Claimed effects**: Weight loss, edema reduction, fluid balance. In trials: patients lost 8–11 pounds per week (discontinued after one month; 40 minutes/day required). Water Bliss oil produced 25 pounds weight loss in four months for one patient; another patient with years of leg swelling experienced reduction using Water Bliss.

**Circuit points**: Spleen 4 (inside foot, behind big toe joint), Conception Vessel 14 (center abdomen, below breastbone), Governing Vessel 8 (center spine, opposite CV 14), Bladder 13 (1 inch each side of spine, at shoulder level), Heart 6 (between two tendons on inside wrist), Bladder 10 (½ inch each side of center spine, just inside skull), Triple Heater 16 (back of neck, turning-point muscle behind mandible), Governing Vessel 20 (crown of head)

**Oil blend**: Water Bliss

## Ring Earth — Calcitonin and Pain

**Biological target**: Calcitonin (produced by thyroid gland)

**Claimed effects**: Pain control (calcitonin is 40–60 times more potent than morphine as analgesic), addiction, phobias, skin disorders. Also described as activating "contact with earth"; helping ALS, Parkinson's, and multiple sclerosis (Shealy specifies Ring Earth + Ring Crystal for these). Raises calcitonin; potentially reverses osteoporosis. Note: thyroid temperature must be 98.6°F; iodine supplementation may be needed first for Ring Fire to regenerate thyroid function.

**Circuit points**: Kidney 1 (soles of feet), Bladder 60 (outside ankle bones), Bladder 54 (center of knees), Large Intestine 16 (shoulder junction), Stomach 9 (throat, beside Adam's apple — pinch only, do not tap), Small Intestine 17 (front edge of neck muscle beside St 9 — pinch), Governing Vessel 20 (crown of head)

**Oil blend**: Earth Bliss

## Ring Crystal — Free Radical Reduction

**Biological target**: Free radical elimination (measured as malondialdehyde in urine)

**Claimed effects**: 85% reduction in free radicals within 3–5 days of GHz stimulation; Crystal Bliss oil achieves 80% reduction long-term. Supports cell regeneration; helps MS and ALS (with Ring Earth). Fruit concentrates reduce free radicals by 43% in comparison; no supplement matches Ring Crystal's results, Shealy claims.

**Circuit points**: Spleen 4 (inside foot), Gallbladder 30.5 (center of femur, bottom of buttock), Conception Vessel 8.5 (1 inch below umbilicus), Governing Vessel 4.5 (center spine, opposite CV 8.5), Conception Vessel 14.5 (center bottom of breastbone), Governing Vessel 7.5 (center spine, opposite CV 14.5), Conception Vessel 23 (top of Adam's apple — pinch only), Governing Vessel 14.4 (center spine), Gallbladder 7 (scalp, where forehead meets scalp in front of ears), Governing Vessel 20 (crown of head)

**Oil blend**: Crystal Bliss

## Telomere Connection

Shealy extends the Sacred Rings framework to longevity through telomere research. Telomeres (the protective end-caps of chromosomes) shrink approximately 1% per year from birth. Shealy claims that:

- GHz stimulation (54–78 GHz, 1 billionth watt/cm²) applied via a RejuvaMatrix mattress can regenerate telomeres at 4.5% per year (from personal 8-year measurement series)
- The Five Bliss Oils (applied for 2.5 minutes daily to the ring circuits) produced 17.36% telomere increase over 7 years vs. an expected 25% decrease — a 42.36% swing

These claims are based on Shealy's personal telomere measurements and a pilot of 30 individuals. Independent replication has not been published.

## William Tiller's Physics Framework

Stanford physicist William Tiller contributes an appendix to *Living Bliss* proposing a theoretical framework for how the Sacred Rings work. In Tiller's model:

- Human beings have multiple levels of organization: the physical atom-molecule layer is the outermost; within it are an emotional domain layer and a mind domain layer (both in "reciprocal space" — faster than light); and at the core is the spirit/God self
- The acupuncture meridian system operates at the thought-form layer of the personality self — not the physical layer — which is why it has no anatomical correlate in conventional anatomy
- The Sacred Rings function through these informational layers, induced by the physical point stimulation but operating at the level of soul-body template

Tiller's model is a theoretical physics extension of the energy medicine framework also articulated in Shealy's earlier work and in [Myss](../thinkers/caroline-myss.md)'s energy anatomy.

## Critique

**What the research supports**: Acupuncture at specific points has demonstrable physiological effects (DHEA changes, endorphin release, autonomic modulation) in some research. GHz-range electromagnetic fields are known to interact with biological tissue. Calcitonin is a genuine analgesic; its release from thyroid stimulation is documented.

**What the research does not yet support**: The specific circuits, the extreme effect sizes (600% neurotensin increase, 85% free radical reduction, 4.5% telomere growth), and the oil-based activation are based entirely on Shealy's own unpublished research. No independent group has replicated these findings. The case reports (autism, PTSD, ADHD reversal) are striking but scientifically insufficient.

**Note**: Mainstream medicine does not recognize the Five Sacred Rings as validated treatments. The theoretical framework (Tiller's biobodysuit, Shealy's intuition-guided point discovery) operates outside empirical science's current methodology. The claims require independent, controlled investigation before clinical application beyond Shealy's own practice.
