# Four Dimensions of Being

**Summary**: Brennan's expanded model of human existence as four nested dimensions — physical world, auric/HEF level, haric level (intentionality), and core star (divine essence) — introduced in *Light Emerging* (1993) to explain why healing at the auric level alone is sometimes insufficient.

**Sources**:
- [Brennan, B. A. (1993). *Light emerging: The journey of personal healing*. Bantam Books.](../sources/light-emerging.md)

**Last updated**: 2026-06-24

---

## Overview

In *[Hands of Light](../sources/hands-of-light.md)* (1987), [Brennan](../thinkers/barbara-ann-brennan.md) describes the human energy system as seven interpenetrating auric layers surrounding the physical body. In *[Light Emerging](../sources/light-emerging.md)* (1993), she extends this model upward and inward, adding two deeper dimensions that *underlie* the seven-layer field:

1. **Physical world** — the material body and its environment
2. **Auric level (HEF)** — the seven-layer Human Energy Field, the domain of personality (thoughts, emotions, relationship, spiritual experience)
3. **Haric level** — the dimension of intentionality and life purpose; the foundation on which the auric field rests
4. **Core star level** — the dimension of divine essence; the source from which all creative energy originates

Each deeper dimension underlies and gives rise to the ones above it. The physical body exists because the auric field organises it; the auric field rests on the hara line's intentional structure; the hara line originates from the core star. This means that healing which addresses only the body or even the auric field may be addressing symptoms rather than causes if the root dysfunction lies at the haric or core star level.

## Dimension 1: Physical World

The material body and physical environment. In Brennan's M-3 metaphysics ([see Holographic Healing Model](holographic-healing-model.md)), the physical world is the most "dense" expression of consciousness — it arises from underlying energy levels rather than giving rise to them. This reverses the M-1 assumption that physical processes are primary.

Physical-level healing tools (medication, surgery, exercise, diet) remain valuable and necessary — Brennan is not anti-medicine — but they address the most downstream level of the causal chain.

## Dimension 2: Auric Level (Human Energy Field)

The [seven-layer auric field](seven-layer-auric-field.md) described in detail in *Hands of Light*:

| Layer | Body | Domain of life experience |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Etheric | Physical sensation; body awareness |
| 2 | Emotional | Feelings; self-relationship |
| 3 | Mental | Thought; clarity; mental patterns |
| 4 | Astral | Relationships; the I–Thou connection |
| 5 | Etheric template | Will; creative patterning; truth |
| 6 | Celestial | Spiritual love; ecstasy; connection |
| 7 | Ketheric template | Universal mind; serenity; pattern perception |

The auric field is the domain of personality. Healing at this level means addressing the needs corresponding to each layer (see [Seven Levels of Healing Process](seven-stages-of-healing.md)).

## Dimension 3: Haric Level

The [hara line](hara-level.md) is the structural correlate of intentionality. It underlies the entire auric field and holds the body in manifestation. Its three key points — the Individuation Point (ID point) above the head, the Soul Seat in the chest, and the Tan Tien below the navel — correspond to:

- **Why one is incarnated** (ID point: reason for being; connection to the divine)
- **What the soul longs for** (Soul Seat: the felt calling, the life task)
- **The will to live in the body** (Tan Tien: the one note that holds form)

Haric-level dysfunction shows as cynicism (disconnected ID point), loss of meaning (shrouded soul seat), or fundamental instability in the body and personality (damaged tan tien). Illness whose origin cannot be found in current-life biographical stress often has haric roots.

## Dimension 4: Core Star Level

The [core star](core-star.md) is the divine individuation — the localised expression of the divine within each person. It is the source of all creative energy, which flows upward through the hara line, into the auric field, and from there into the body and physical life.

When the core star is active and unobstructed, creative energy wells up through all four dimensions and expresses as health, creativity, joy, and aliveness. When it is shrouded or disconnected, the person loses contact with their own divinity and cannot source the energy needed for authentic healing.

The core star is not the same as the Higher Self in the personality sense — it is the pre-personality ground of being, the divine spark that has never been tainted by biographical experience.

## The Creative Process as Healing

Brennan frames healing in terms of this four-dimensional creative process:

> "The process originates from the core, begins with two ingredients: intention and the experience of pleasure. Everything that has ever been done in life began with these two. The act begins from the core; consciousness upwelled through the levels and reached the world."

The direction of healthy creative flow is: **Core Star → Hara Line → Auric Field → Physical World**.

Illness arises when this flow is blocked at any level:
- Physical blocking: poor self-care; ignoring the body's signals
- Auric blocking: unresolved emotional patterns, negative thought forms, relational dysfunction
- Haric blocking: misaligned intention; unknown or rejected life purpose
- Core star blocking: disconnection from divine essence; forgetting one's nature

Healing reverses the direction in a sense: the healer helps unblock the field and hara, then facilitates the upwelling of core essence — which then floods through all dimensions outward to the physical level.

## Relationship to Other Frameworks

**Myss's seven-chakra model**: Myss's chakras correspond to Brennan's auric levels — they work at the same dimensional plane. Brennan's four-dimension model adds the haric and core star dimensions that Myss does not explicitly map.

**Hillman's acorn theory**: The "acorn" — the soul's unique calling — corresponds closely to what Brennan locates in the Soul Seat at the haric level: the longing that leads to the life task. Both frameworks assert a pre-biographical soul-level intention for the life.

**Myss's Sacred Contracts**: The pre-birth soul agreements of *Sacred Contracts* can be understood as haric-level intentions — the purposes chosen before incarnation that the haric line's ID point carries as the "reason for being."

## Scientific Grounding

Brennan observes the haric level and core star entirely through [High Sense Perception (HSP)](high-sense-perception.md). No instruments have independently measured these dimensions. The haric level is described as existing in a "higher dimension" than the auric field — beyond the four-dimensional spacetime that current instruments can access.

**Note**: Mainstream science and medicine do not recognise the haric level or core star as anatomical or physical realities. These are phenomenological descriptions derived from extended perceptual observation, not empirically validated structures.
