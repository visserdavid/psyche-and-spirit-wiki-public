# Seven-Layer Auric Field

**Summary**: Barbara Ann Brennan's detailed model of the Human Energy Field (HEF) as seven distinct interpenetrating bodies, each extending further from the physical body, vibrating at a higher frequency, and corresponding to one of the seven chakras — the foundational anatomy of energy healing as she practises and teaches it.

- [Brennan, B. A. (1987). *Hands of light: A guide to healing through the human energy field*. Pleiades Books.](../sources/hands-of-light.md)

## Overview

[Brennan](../thinkers/barbara-ann-brennan.md) describes the Human Energy Field (HEF) not as a single undifferentiated aura but as seven distinct interpenetrating bodies. Each body:

- Extends further from the physical body than the one below it
- Vibrates at a higher frequency than the one below it
- Corresponds to one of the [seven chakras](chakra-system.md)
- Contains the full anatomical structure of the physical body in energetic form (organs, vessels, nerves)
- Can be perceived and worked with by a sufficiently developed healer

The seven bodies are nested within each other like Russian dolls but are not concentric shells — they interpenetrate. The physical body is embedded within all seven energy bodies, each of which extends outward to its own range. Disease first appears in the HEF before manifesting in the physical body; healing the HEF restores the template from which the physical body can reorganise.

The model draws on and systematises earlier observations by Rosalyn Bruyere (Healing Light Center, Glendale, California) and the Theosophical tradition (Leadbeater, Powell), while adding Brennan's own fifteen years of clairvoyant observation and her collaborators' laboratory measurements.

## The Seven Layers

### Layer 1: Etheric Body
- **Extent**: ¼ to 2 inches from the skin
- **Appearance**: A sparkling web of fine blue-gray or bluish-white lines of light, resembling a television screen or luminous spiderweb; pulses at 15–20 cycles per minute in wave motion along the arms, legs, and torso
- **Chakra correspondence**: Chakra 1 (Root)
- **Function**: The matrix template for the physical body; tissues grow along and are anchored by its grid structure. John Pierrakos observed that the etheric energy field of plant seeds already shows the shape of the mature plant before physical growth occurs.
- **Key property**: The etheric body is the energetic blueprint whose grid lines form the scaffold along which physical cells organise

### Layer 2: Emotional Body
- **Extent**: 1 to 3 inches from the body
- **Appearance**: Coloured clouds of substance in constant motion; does not duplicate the body's structure but swirls through it; contains all the colours of the rainbow
- **Chakra correspondence**: Chakra 2 (Sacral)
- **Function**: Carries feelings and emotional life; colours correspond to emotional quality — clear bright hues indicate healthy emotional flow, dark muddied hues indicate confused or suppressed feeling
- **Observable phenomena**: During therapy sessions, bursts of colour shoot from the body when emotions are expressed; anger appears as red bolts; withheld anger produces dark inward spots; drugs (cocaine, LSD) produce visible "mucus" clouds that persist for days

### Layer 3: Mental Body
- **Extent**: 3 to 8 inches from the body (expands when the owner concentrates)
- **Appearance**: A bright yellow-gold light radiating principally from head and shoulders; contains thought forms appearing as bright blobs of light with colour overlays representing the emotional valence of the thought
- **Chakra correspondence**: Chakra 3 (Solar Plexus)
- **Function**: Carries thought processes; thought forms are energy-consciousness structures that, once formed and energised, have an influence in the world. Mental processes directed by will create forms that can be sustained or released; unexamined thought forms can linger in the field and perpetuate patterns of experience

### Layer 4: Astral Body
- **Extent**: ½ to 1 foot from the body
- **Appearance**: Clouds of colour similar to the emotional body but infused with the rose light of love; particularly vivid around the heart centre; contains cords connecting the person to others
- **Chakra correspondence**: Chakra 4 (Heart)
- **Function**: The medium of heart connection; the interface where personal love meets universal love. Relationship cords form between chakras of people in close relationship; when relationships end or shift, these cords are torn, producing real energetic pain. People in love show rose-coloured arcs between their heart chakras. The astral body is the doorway to the higher planes of existence.

### Layer 5: Etheric Template Body
- **Extent**: 1 to 2 feet from the body
- **Appearance**: Resembles a photographic negative — form defined by the space that is not filled; lines and structures appear in the background as the negative space around the physical form
- **Chakra correspondence**: Chakra 5 (Throat)
- **Function**: The template from which the etheric body derives its structure. Brennan describes this as the level at which sound creates matter — sounding (toning, chant) is the healing modality for this layer. Spiritual surgery and restructuring of the physical body's etheric matrix is performed at this level.
- **Healing application**: When physical tissues have been damaged or removed (e.g., post-surgery), their etheric template remains at this level and can be worked with to support regeneration

### Layer 6: Celestial Body
- **Extent**: 2 to 2¾ feet from the body
- **Appearance**: Opalescent mother-of-pearl light radiating from the body; composed of gold, silver, and pastel colours with a shimmering, sequin-like quality
- **Chakra correspondence**: Chakra 6 (Third Eye)
- **Function**: The vehicle of spiritual ecstasy; the level at which the healer experiences union with the Divine and universal love. Brennan describes states of expanded consciousness at this level as necessary for healing the celestial layer — the healer must enter a state of deep love and unity before this layer can be touched. This is the level of devotional spiritual practices.

### Layer 7: Ketheric Template / Causal Body
- **Extent**: 2½ to 3½ feet from the body (varies with the person's energetic development)
- **Appearance**: An egg-shaped golden body composed of thousands of threads of gold and silver light, extremely resilient and durable, holding the form of the entire auric field. The main vertical power current runs through its centre. Life bands — coloured bands of light encircling the egg, one per incarnation — encode the lessons and themes of past lifetimes.
- **Chakra correspondence**: Chakra 7 (Crown)
- **Function**: The causal body and life plan. Contains the blueprint for the entire HEF and thus for the physical body; holds the soul's life purpose and the lessons chosen for this incarnation. The ketheric template is the level at which past-life trauma is encoded as life bands. It is the last layer to be healed in the healing sequence, and sealing it completes the work.

## The Chakra-Layer Correspondence

Each chakra is not simply a single vortex but a seven-level structure — one chakra at each layer of the HEF. The first chakra at the etheric level handles physical survival energy; the first chakra at the ketheric level handles the soul's ground of being. The energy flowing through a chakra at its highest level must pass downward through all seven layers to reach the physical body.

Front aspects of chakras relate to feelings; back aspects relate to will. The paired front-back chakras together constitute the full functioning of that centre. Many people have well-developed front chakras (feelings accessible) but underdeveloped back chakras (will weak), or vice versa.

The main vertical power current connects all chakra roots along the spine, from root chakra to crown. Secondary horizontal currents radiate outward at each chakra level, forming the basket-weave structure of the ketheric egg.

## Scientific Grounding

Laboratory evidence Brennan cites for the HEF:

- **Burr & Northrop** (Yale, 1939): measuring the L-field of plant seeds predicted mature plant structure; mapped the DC field of frog eggs and located the nervous system; detected ovulation in women
- **Ravitz** (William & Mary, 1959): HEF fluctuates with mental state; thought processes alter the field measurably
- **Inyushin** (Kazakh University): bioplasma — a fifth state of matter, a charged plasma of ions and electrons — as the physical substrate of the HEF
- **Hunt & Bruyere** (UCLA, 1977): during rolfing sessions, Bruyere's real-time auric colour observations correlated with instrument-measured body-surface frequencies. Colour-frequency relationships: blue/violet 250–400 Hz; green 250–475 Hz; yellow 500–700 Hz; orange 950–1050 Hz; red 1000–1200 Hz; violet 1000–2000 Hz; white 1100–2000 Hz. These frequency bands do not replicate visible-spectrum colour order, suggesting the eye-brain system processes field frequencies into colour experiences through its own encoding scheme.
- **SQUID** (superconducting quantum interference device): measures body field without contact; provides information about brain state beyond EEG
- **Sheldrake's [morphogenetic fields](morphic-resonance.md)**: organizing fields that shape form and behaviour beyond known material processes — parallel to but distinct from the HEF; unlike Brennan's field, whose organisation Brennan claims to observe directly via clairvoyant perception, Sheldrake's field is proposed to act via a mechanism (morphic resonance) intended to be tested indirectly through statistical, population-level effects, and remains classified as pseudoscience by mainstream biology (see [Morphic Resonance § Reception and Critique](morphic-resonance.md#reception-and-critique))

## Light Emerging: Extended Layer Descriptions and the Fourth Dimension

*[Light Emerging](../sources/light-emerging.md)* (1993) deepens the layer descriptions in Chapter 2 — providing much richer accounts of the life-experience domains each layer governs — and extends the model beyond the seven layers to a [four-dimensional framework](four-dimensions-of-being.md).

**Enriched layer domains** (Brennan, 1993, Chapter 2):

| Layer | Hands of Light emphasis | Light Emerging emphasis |
|---|---|---|
| 1 (Etheric) | Physical matrix template | The body's ability to experience pleasure and physical sensation |
| 2 (Emotional) | Feelings | Self-acceptance; the full range of emotional response to the self |
| 3 (Mental) | Thought forms | Rational thinking; linear perception; the mind's relationship to clarity |
| 4 (Astral/Relational) | Heart connection, cords | All loving interaction with people, creatures, the world |
| 5 (Etheric template) | Sound and blueprint | Alignment between will and truth; speaking what one actually means |
| 6 (Celestial) | Spiritual ecstasy | Experience of divine love; spiritual connection; inner light |
| 7 (Ketheric) | Life plan, past-life bands | Integration of the higher mind; serenity; perception of the whole |

**The fourth dimension**: The seven layers are not the deepest level. In *Light Emerging*, Brennan introduces the [haric level](hara-level.md) (the dimension of intentionality, with its hara line and three key points: Individuation Point, Soul Seat, Tan Tien) and the [core star](core-star.md) (the divine essence). Together, these form a four-level nested model in which the seven-layer HEF rests on the haric foundation, which rests on the core star. Creative energy flows downward: Core Star → Hara Line → Auric Field → Physical Body.

This extension addresses a clinical gap in the *Hands of Light* model: some illnesses whose root lies at the haric level (disconnection from life purpose) do not respond sustainably to auric-level healing alone.

## Relationship to Other Frameworks

**Myss's energy anatomy**: Myss describes the HEF as a biographical record of experience held in the [chakra system](chakra-system.md). Brennan provides the seven-layer structural anatomy that gives this claim specific, spatially locatable content. Where Myss reads the field for biographical and symbolic meaning, Brennan reads it anatomically and restructures it therapeutically.

**Keleman's somatic layering**: [Keleman](../thinkers/stanley-keleman.md) describes the body's three embryonic tissue layers (ectoderm, mesoderm, endoderm) as encoding biographical experience in physical structure — a materialist parallel to Brennan's seven-layer HEF. Both claim that biography is encoded in the body's layers; they differ only in whether the layers are anatomically physical or energetically subtle.

**Shealy's bioelectromagnetism**: [Shealy](../thinkers/norman-shealy.md) and [Becker](../thinkers/robert-becker.md) provide independent laboratory evidence for measurable bioelectric and bioelectromagnetic fields surrounding living organisms. This is consistent with Brennan's HEF model but does not confirm the seven-layer structure specifically.

**Theosophical tradition**: The seven-body system (physical, etheric, astral, mental, causal, buddhic, atmic) from Blavatsky, Leadbeater, and Steiner is the closest precursor to Brennan's model. Brennan adopts the seven-layer structure while adapting the content from her own direct observation and the Bruyere/Pierrakos tradition.

## Critique

**Observational basis**: The seven-layer model rests on clairvoyant observation by Brennan and a small number of other sensitives (Bruyere, Karagulla, Kunz). These observations are consistent with each other but have not been independently verified through laboratory instrumentation for all seven layers. The Hunt/Bruyere UCLA measurements provide partial support for auric colour-frequency correlations but do not confirm the full seven-layer anatomy.

**The bioplasma hypothesis**: Inyushin's bioplasma model has not been confirmed by subsequent Western research. Soviet/Russian parapsychological research of the 1960s–1980s was not fully peer-reviewed by international standards.

**Note**: Mainstream physics and medicine do not recognise the seven-layer HEF as a described or measured structure. The bioelectric fields that mainstream science measures (ECG, EEG, SQUID) are understood as byproducts of ionic processes in tissue, not as structured information-carrying bodies corresponding to psychological and spiritual functions.
